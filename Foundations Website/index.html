Who are we?

Upakar Foundation Nepal or also known as (UFN).We are a non profitable organization established on Poush 12,2074.This organization was established to provide the free services (Health,Education)to the poor and needy people and provide awareness in our society.We believe that we all Nepalese are a family and one should come ahead in the need of other.So we are here with the slogan "Maanav Hit,Haamro Jit."
